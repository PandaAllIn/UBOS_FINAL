## Overview

The EU Funding Management System (EUFM) is NOT a standalone system. It's deeply integrated with the broader UBOS (Universal Blueprint Operating System) architecture, constitutional framework, and operational infrastructure.

**Philosophy:** EUFM implements the Recursive Enhancement Protocol - every funding pursuit strengthens the overall system.

**EUFM supports strategic objectives defined in:**
- `01_STRATEGY/ROADMAP.md` - Phase 2.6 (Autonomous Vessel Protocol) and beyond

## Conflict Resolution

**If EUFM priorities conflict with other UBOS priorities:**

1. **Consult Strategic Roadmap:** What's current phase priority?
2. **Evaluate Cascade Effects:** Does funding enable or block other work?
3. **Calculate Opportunity Cost:** What do we sacrifice by pursuing/skipping?
4. **Constitutional Test:** Which option better serves Lion's Sanctuary mission?
5. **Captain Decision:** Final call rests with strategic leadership

**General Principle:** Funding is a means, not an end. If pursuing grant detracts from mission, skip it.
