## Timeline Categories

### IMMEDIATE (<30 days to deadline)
**Action:** Drop everything, focus here  
**Risk:** Miss deadline = zero chance  
**Effort:** Maximum intensity

### SHORT-TERM (1-3 months to deadline)
**Action:** Active preparation  
**Risk:** Delays compound quickly  
**Effort:** Steady progress

### MEDIUM-TERM (3-6 months to deadline)
**Action:** Strategic planning  
**Risk:** False sense of security  
**Effort:** Foundation building

### LONG-TERM (6+ months to deadline)
**Action:** Partner cultivation  
**Risk:** Forget about it  
**Effort:** Relationship development

## Timeline Workflow

### Weekly Review (Every Monday)

1. **Update deadlines** - Check all portals for changes
2. **Move opportunities** - Shift between categories as time passes
3. **Assess status** - Are we on track? Falling behind?
4. **Reallocate resources** - Adjust effort based on urgency
5. **Flag risks** - What's at risk of missing deadline?

### Monthly Strategic Review

1. **Portfolio balance** - Do we have right mix of timelines?
2. **Success probability** - Are we pursuing winnable opportunities?
3. **Resource allocation** - Is effort matching opportunity value?
4. **Learning capture** - What did completed applications teach us?
