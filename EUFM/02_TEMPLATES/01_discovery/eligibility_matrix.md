## GO / NO-GO DECISION

**Decision Gate:**

✅ **GO** if:
- Zero ❌ BLOCKERS
- All ⚠️ GAPS are fixable within timeline and budget
- Opportunity score ≥2.8
- Strategic alignment confirmed

❌ **NO-GO** if:
- Any ❌ BLOCKER exists
- ⚠️ GAPS cannot be closed in time
- Opportunity score <2.8
- Misalignment with strategy

**FINAL DECISION:** 
- [ ] **GO** - Proceed to Phase 3 (Documentation)
- [ ] **FIX GAPS FIRST** - Address ⚠️ items, then re-evaluate
- [ ] **NO-GO** - Skip this opportunity, document reasons
