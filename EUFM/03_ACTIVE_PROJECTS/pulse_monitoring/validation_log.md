## Validation Methodology

### How We Measure Success

**Key Metrics:**
1. **Lead Time:** Days between our detection and general public awareness
2. **Accuracy:** Predicted outcome vs. actual outcome match rate
3. **False Positive Rate:** Signals that didn't materialize / Total signals
4. **False Negative Rate:** Opportunities we missed / Total relevant opportunities

**Target Performance (End of Phase 1):**
- Average Lead Time: 45-60 days
- Prediction Accuracy: >75%
- False Positive Rate: <15%
- False Negative Rate: <5%
