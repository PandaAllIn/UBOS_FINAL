## Validation Methodology

For each detected signal:
1. **Log detection:** Date, source, confidence level
2. **Make prediction:** Expected outcome (call publication, budget, timeline)
3. **Track reality:** What actually happened
4. **Calculate accuracy:** Predicted vs. actual (days difference)
5. **Update model:** Refine thresholds based on performance

**Target Metrics:**
- Average lead time: 45-60 days before general awareness
- Prediction accuracy: >75%
- False positive rate: <15%
- Missed opportunities: <5%
