## Application Process

### Consortium Requirements

**Typical Structure:**
- Minimum 3 partners from 3 different EU member states
- Public-private partnerships encouraged
- SME participation welcomed
- Academic-industry collaboration valued

**For AI Factories:**
- Lead: Technical infrastructure provider
- Partners: End-user companies, research institutions
- Romanian advantage: Can be lead or strong partner

### Evaluation Criteria

**Excellence (weighted ~40%):**
- Innovation and ambition
- Technical soundness
- State-of-the-art advancement

**Impact (weighted ~30%):**
- European added value
- Deployment and scalability
- Economic and societal benefits

**Implementation (weighted ~30%):**
- Work plan quality
- Consortium capabilities
- Resource allocation
