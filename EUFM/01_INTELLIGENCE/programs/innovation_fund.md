## Application Process

### Call Structure

**Annual Large-Scale Calls:**
- Publication: Q4 of previous year
- Deadline: Q3 of application year
- Evaluation: 6-9 months
- Contract: 3-6 months after approval

**Two-Stage Process:**

**Stage 1: Expression of Interest (EoI)**
- Lightweight submission
- Pre-screening for eligibility
- ~40% advance to Stage 2

**Stage 2: Full Application**
- Detailed project proposal
- Financial model
- Technical specifications
- GHG calculation methodology
- Risk assessment
- Implementation plan
