# PNRR Romania - Digitalization Grants for SMEs

**Programme:** Plan National de Redresare si Rezilienta (PNRR)  
**Component:** Digitalization of SMEs  
**Managing Authority:** Ministry of European Investments and Projects (Romania)  
**Status:** ACTIVE - Rolling submissions

---

## Quick Facts

**Total Budget:** €347.5 million (Romania digitalization component)  
**Grant Size:** €20,000 - €100,000 per SME  
**Funding Rate:** 90% non-reimbursable (10% co-financing required)  
**Timeline:** Rolling applications through 2025-2026  
**Success Rate:** ~70% for eligible applicants (high!)

---

## Programme Objective

Support Romanian SMEs in adopting digital technologies and advanced ICT solutions to increase competitiveness and productivity.

---

## Eligibility Criteria

### Applicant Requirements

✅ **MUST MEET ALL:**
- Legal entity registered in Romania
- Classified as SME (micro, small, or medium enterprise)
- Company registered BEFORE December 31, 2021
- Activity NOT interrupted in 2022
- Operating profit >0 in 2022
- Not in difficulty (as defined by EU rules)

❌ **CANNOT APPLY IF:**
- Main CAEN code is IT consultancy (6201, 6202, 6209)
- Involved in gambling, weapons, tobacco
- Has outstanding debts to Romanian state
- Previously received de minimis aid exceeding limits

### Project Requirements

**Eligible Expenses:**
- ICT hardware equipment (servers, workstations, networking, etc.)
- Software licenses (operating systems, business applications)
- IoT devices and sensors
- Automation and robotics equipment integrated with digital solutions
- Blockchain technology infrastructure
- Installation, configuration, and commissioning
- Staff training for ICT equipment operation (max 10% of total)

**Ineligible Expenses:**
- Used/second-hand equipment
- Leasing or rental (must be purchase)
- Operating costs
- Marketing and advertising
- Physical infrastructure not related to digitalization

---

## Evaluation & Scoring

### DESI (Digital Economy and Society Index) Criteria

**MUST achieve minimum 6 of these after project completion:**

1. >50% of employees use computers with internet access
2. Using ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) software
3. Internet connection ≥30 Mbps
4. Online sales represent >1% of total turnover
5. Using Internet of Things (IoT) technologies
6. Active presence on social networks
7. Using Cloud Computing services
8. Using Big Data analytics
9. E-commerce platform operational
10. Using AI/Machine Learning tools

**Strategy:** Choose the 6 easiest to achieve for your situation. Document how project enables them.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Preparation

**Documents Needed:**
- Company registration certificate (Certificat de Înregistrare)
- Financial statements 2022 (Bilanț Contabil)
- Tax clearance certificate (Certificat Fiscal)
- 2 price quotations from suppliers
- Declaration of de minimis aid received
- Declaration of honor (template provided)
- Staff training plan (if including training)

### Step 2: Submission

**Portal:** https://proiecte.pnrr.gov.ro/  
**Platform:** MySMIS2021  
**Language:** Romanian

**Timeline:**
- Portal registration: 1-2 days
- Document upload: 2-3 hours
- Submit at least 48 hours before internal deadline

### Step 3: Evaluation

**Timeline:** 60-90 days typically

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Legal eligibility (pass/fail)
- Financial viability (pass/fail)
- Project completeness (pass/fail)
- DESI alignment (scoring)

**Result:** Approved, Rejected, or Request for Clarification

### Step 4: Contract & Implementation

**If Approved:**
- Contract signing within 30 days
- Implementation period: 12-18 months typically
- Reimbursement: After completion or staged
- Reporting: Initial, interim, final

## Financial Details

### Budget Structure

**Example: €20,000 Project**
- EU Grant: €18,000 (90%)
- Your Co-financing: €2,000 (10%)

**Breakdown:**
```
Hardware:         €15,000 (75%)
Software:         €3,000  (15%)
Training:         €2,000  (10% - maximum allowed)
TOTAL:           €20,000
```

### Payment Schedule

**Option A: Reimbursement**
- You pay suppliers upfront
- Submit invoices and proof of payment
- Receive 90% reimbursement within 60-90 days

**Option B: Direct Payment (if available)**
- Managing authority pays suppliers directly
- You pay only 10% co-financing

**Confirm:** Check current payment modality with managing authority

## Success Factors

### High Probability Elements

✅ **Clear hardware needs** - Specific equipment for specific business purpose  
✅ **DESI alignment** - Show how you'll achieve 6+ criteria  
✅ **Training plan** - Demonstrates serious implementation intent  
✅ **Supplier quotes** - From authorized distributors, detailed specifications  
✅ **Financial health** - Positive 2022 results, clean tax status

### Common Rejection Reasons

❌ Ineligible CAEN code (main activity is IT consultancy)  
❌ Company too new (registered after Dec 31, 2021)  
❌ Negative operating profit in 2022  
❌ De minimis aid limit exceeded  
❌ Incomplete documentation  
❌ Used/second-hand equipment proposed  
❌ Weak connection to DESI criteria
