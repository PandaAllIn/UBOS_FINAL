## Primary EU Portals

### Funding & Tenders Portal (Main Entry Point)
**URL:** https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/screen/home  
**Purpose:** Central access point for ALL EU funding programmes  
**Coverage:** Horizon Europe, Digital Europe, LIFE, etc.  
**Features:**
- Opportunity search
- Application submission
- Project management
- Reporting

**Registration Required:** YES (EU Login + PIC code)

---

### EU Login (Authentication)
**URL:** https://webgate.ec.europa.eu/cas/  
**Purpose:** Single sign-on for all EU systems  
**Required For:** Portal access, submissions, reporting

**Setup Instructions:**
1. Create EU Login account
2. Verify email
3. Enable two-factor authentication (recommended)
4. Keep credentials secure

---

### PIC (Participant Identification Code)
**URL:** https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/screen/how-to-participate/participant-register  
**Purpose:** Unique organization identifier for EU funding  
**Required For:** All grant applications

**How to Get PIC:**
1. Register organization on Funding & Tenders Portal
2. Provide legal and financial information
3. Receive 9-digit PIC code
4. Update annually
