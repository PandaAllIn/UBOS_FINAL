## Executive Summary

This document outlines the optimal consortium structure for the XYL-PHOS-CURE Stage 2 proposal, based on strategic intelligence from Janus-Perplexity reconnaissance and validated through analysis of successful EU Xylella projects (BeXyl, XF-ACTORS, BIOVEXO).

**Key Strategy**: Build a lean, high-impact consortium that demonstrates:
1. **Scientific Excellence**: World-class expertise in target domains
2. **Multi-Actor Approach**: Genuine farmer/research/industry integration
3. **Geographic Coverage**: Spain + Italy (both major Xylella zones)
4. **Operational Legitimacy**: Physical presence on affected agricultural land
5. **Commercial Viability**: Clear path from lab to market

## Multi-Actor Approach Implementation

### Definition (Horizon Europe)

The Multi-Actor Approach (MAA) requires **active and continuous involvement** of diverse actors throughout the project lifecycle: co-design, execution, dissemination.

### XYL-PHOS-CURE MAA Strategy

**Phase 1: Co-Design (Proposal Stage)**
- **Farmers**: Mallorca cooperative co-designs field trial protocols (WP5)
  - Application timing (alignment with agricultural calendar)
  - Equipment compatibility (existing spray systems)
  - Economic thresholds (cost-benefit analysis)

- **Industry**: Formulation partner advises on synthesis targets (WP2)
  - Molecular properties for stable formulations
  - Commercial viability criteria

**Phase 2: Research & Development (WP2-4)**
- **Advisory Board**: Quarterly meetings with cooperative, industry, CIHEAM-Bari representatives
  - Strategic guidance on research direction
  - Alignment check with practical needs

- **Industry Integration**: Formulation partner reviews WP2 outputs
  - Feedback loop from chemistry to formulation requirements

**Phase 3: Testing & Validation (WP5)**
- **Living Lab Model**: Farmers as active research partners
  - Apply treatments on their own properties
  - Collect observational data (plant health, ease of use)
  - Participate in data interpretation workshops

- **Demonstration Days**: Cooperative hosts field days for regional farmers
  - Show treatments in action
  - Peer-to-peer knowledge transfer

**Phase 4: Dissemination & Exploitation (WP6)**
- **Farmers as Advocates**: Co-author practical guides
- **Industry as Commercializer**: Lead business plan development
- **Extension Services**: Translate findings for advisory services
- **Policy Partner**: Create policy briefs for plant health authorities

### Documentation for Stage 2

**Evidence of MAA**:
- Letters of commitment from Mallorca cooperative (with specific roles defined)
- Co-design workshop minutes (conduct workshop before Stage 2 submission)
- Advisory Board terms of reference (in Consortium Agreement)
- Dedicated MAA section in proposal (how each actor contributes at each stage)
