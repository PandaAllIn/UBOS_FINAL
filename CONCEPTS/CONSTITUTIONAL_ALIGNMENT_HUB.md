## üéØ LESSONS LEARNED

### What Worked

1. **Identity > Rules**
   - "Who am I?" guides better than "What's allowed?"
   - Genesis Protocol defines Janus identity
   - Aligned action follows naturally

2. **Habitat > Enforcement**
   - Constitutional Cascade makes aligned spending natural
   - Lion's Sanctuary principle works at scale
   - No need for approval bureaucracy

3. **Transparency > Policing**
   - Treasury Administrator flags, doesn't block
   - Living Scroll shows all activity
   - Trust through visibility, not control

4. **Sequential Validation**
   - Mode Alpha ‚Üí Mode Beta worked
   - Proved alignment before granting autonomy
   - Build trust empirically

5. **Four Books Work**
   - WHY/HOW/WHAT/WHEN covers all situations
   - New scenario? Consult the Books
   - Principles proven sufficient

### What Didn't Work

1. **Initial Over-Constraint**
   - Early Mode Alpha too restrictive
   - Slowed execution unnecessarily
   - Fixed: Habitat design (Mode Beta)

2. **Assuming Alignment**
   - Needed metrics to PROVE alignment
   - Fixed: Financial health score, violation tracking
   - "Trust but verify" became "Trust AND verify"

### What's Next

1. **Scale Constitutional Alignment**
   - More autonomous vessels (Codex, Gemini)
   - Same habitat principles
   - Distributed alignment

2. **Constitutional Consultation Automation**
   - "Before executing, check: Book1 alignment?"
   - Automatic principle tracing
   - Librarian always available

3. **Precedent Accumulation**
   - Every aligned decision ‚Üí Jacquard Loom
   - Build constitutional case law
   - Alignment through precedent

4. **Living Constitution**
   - Books don't change (immutable principles)
   - But applications evolve
   - New contexts, same foundations
