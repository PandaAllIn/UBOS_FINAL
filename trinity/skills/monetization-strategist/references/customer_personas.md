# EUFM Customer Personas

## Navigator (Solo Consultant)
- Revenue €30-100K, budget €200-300/mo, values automation + deadlines.

## Orchestrator (Boutique Consultancy)
- 5-10 staff, revenue €500K-2M, budget €500-1,000/mo, needs multi-seat + integrations.

## Guardian (University Research Office)
- Supports 50-200 researchers, budget €2K-5K/mo, prioritises compliance + dashboards.

## Catalyst (Regional Development Agency)
- Public sector, budget €1K-3K/mo with procurement, needs transparency + reporting.
