# Narrative Templates

## 1. Excellence (Suggested Outline)
1.1 Objectives and ambition (3-4 subsections)
1.2 Methodology and approach (work packages, tasks, deliverables)
1.3 Concept and innovation (state-of-the-art comparison)
1.4 Research infrastructure and resources

## 2. Impact
2.1 Project impact pathways (logic model, KPIs)
2.2 Measures to maximise impact (exploitation, dissemination, communications)
2.3 Target audiences and engagement plan
2.4 Sustainability and scalability

## 3. Implementation
3.1 Work plan (table of WPs, tasks, deliverables, milestones)
3.2 Management structure (decision processes, governance)
3.3 Consortium fit (expertise, partner roles, capacity)
3.4 Risk management and ethics

### Writing Guidance
- Keep to page limits (Section 1: 10 pages, Section 2: 7 pages, Section 3: 10 pages).
- Use numbered headings matching call template.
- Reference EU policies (Green Deal, Digital decade, AI Act) where relevant.
- Cite evidence for economic/social/environmental impacts.
