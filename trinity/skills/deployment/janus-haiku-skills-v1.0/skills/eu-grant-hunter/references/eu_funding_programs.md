# EU FUNDING PROGRAMS OVERVIEW

## Horizon Europe
Focus Areas: Research and innovation across climate, energy, digital transition, health, and agriculture.
Budget Range: €2M–€20M per collaborative project; flagship calls reach €50M+.
Application Flow: Two-stage process (proposal outline then full submission) with strict consortium requirements.
Evaluation Criteria: Excellence, impact, quality of implementation, and European added value.
Keywords: horizon europe, research innovation, consortium, excellence, impact

## European Regional Development Fund (ERDF)
Focus Areas: Regional competitiveness, infrastructure, green transition, and SME support administered by member states.
Budget Range: €1M–€15M typical allocations; co-funding requirements vary by region.
Application Flow: Managed at national or regional level, often via operational programmes with rolling deadlines.
Evaluation Criteria: Regional impact, job creation, sustainability, and alignment with Smart Specialisation Strategies.
Keywords: erdf, regional development, infrastructure, cohesion policy, smart specialisation

## Digital Europe Programme
Focus Areas: High-performance computing, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and advanced digital skills.
Budget Range: €2M–€10M per project with options for procurement or grants.
Application Flow: Centralised EU portal with topic-specific work programmes and annual calls.
Evaluation Criteria: European digital capacity building, interoperability, industrial partnerships, and reusability.
Keywords: digital europe, hpc, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, digital skills

## Innovation Fund
Focus Areas: Large-scale decarbonisation, clean tech deployment, and industrial transformation (ETS-backed).
Budget Range: €7.5M–€300M covering up to 60% of additional capital and operational costs.
Application Flow: Expression of interest followed by full application with rigorous financial modelling.
Evaluation Criteria: Greenhouse gas avoidance, degree of innovation, project maturity, scalability, and cost efficiency.
Keywords: innovation fund, decarbonisation, clean energy, industrial transformation, climate action

## LIFE Programme (Synergy Target)
Focus Areas: Environment, biodiversity, circular economy, and climate adaptation projects complementing Horizon Europe.
Budget Range: €1M–€10M with co-funding between 55–75% of eligible costs.
Application Flow: Concept note submission followed by full proposal upon invitation.
Evaluation Criteria: Environmental impact, EU policy contribution, replicability, and cost effectiveness.
Keywords: life programme, biodiversity, circular economy, climate adaptation
