# Horizon Europe Cost Eligibility Rules

## Direct Costs
- Personnel: actual salaries + social security, timesheets or unit cost options.
- Subcontracting: exceptional, requires justification, no profit margin.
- Equipment: only depreciation during project lifetime; include procurement proof.
- Travel/subsistence: actual costs, economy class, per-diem guidelines.
- Consumables: directly attributable to project.

## Indirect Costs
- Flat rate 25% of eligible direct costs (excluding subcontracting and unit costs).

## Ineligible Costs
- Return on capital, debt interest, provisions for losses, exchange losses.
- Costs declared or reimbursed under another EU grant.

## Budget Best Practices
- Dissemination & communication typically 5% (UBOS target 6-8% to show commitment).
- Management & coordination ~7-10% of total budget.
- Partner field activities: compensate farmers/NGOs fairly with transparent rates.
