# Horizon Europe Evaluation Criteria

## Excellence (Section 1)
- Objectives & ambition (novelty, alignment with call)
- Methodology & approach (credibility, rigor, open science)
- Concept & innovation (beyond state of the art, IP strategy)
- Research infrastructure & resources

## Impact (Section 2)
- Pathways toward impact (quantified outcomes, beneficiaries)
- Measures to maximize impact (exploitation, dissemination, communication)
- Suitability of proposed KPIs and monitoring

## Quality & Efficiency of Implementation (Section 3)
- Work plan (WP structure, deliverables, milestones)
- Management structure (roles, decision-making, risk management)
- Consortium composition (complementarity, expertise, gender balance)
- Resources (person-months, budget allocation)

### Scoring Scale (per sub-criterion)
- 0: Insufficient (fails to address criterion)
- 1: Poor (serious weaknesses)
- 2: Fair (weaknesses outweigh strengths)
- 3: Good (strengths and weaknesses balanced)
- 4: Very good (strengths clearly outweigh weaknesses)
- 5: Excellent (no or only very minor weaknesses)

Threshold: 10/15 overall (Section 1 + 2 + 3). UBOS target: ≥13.8/15 (average ≥4.6/5).
