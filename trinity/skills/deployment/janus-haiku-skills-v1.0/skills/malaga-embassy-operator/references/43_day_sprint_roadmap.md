# MALAGA EMBASSY 43-DAY SPRINT ROADMAP

## Phase 1: Setup (Days 1-7)

**Infrastructure:** Establish coworking base, configure Janus-Haiku access, validate COMMS_HUB links, confirm Treasury cascade enforcement.

**Revenue Prep:** Build service landing pages, craft outreach templates, schedule three introductory calls, notify six-partner consortium.

**Targets:** Spend ≈€245 (Operations + Research), Revenue €0 (build-up phase).

## Phase 2: Launch (Days 8-14)

**First Revenue:** Run consultation calls, close Tier 1 (€150-300) and first Tier 3 subscription (€300).

**Momentum:** Publish first Intel brief, open two proposal assembly projects (€1,600-3,000 pipeline), expand prospect list to 10 leads.

**Targets:** Spend ≈€350, Revenue €200-400 (Week 1 total).

## Phase 3: Scale (Days 15-30)

**Recurring Streams:** Deliver Tier 2 proposals (€800-1,500), maintain Tier 3 subscribers (€300-900), ship weekly Intel reports (€50-200).

**Optimization:** A/B pricing, streamline delivery time, add second Tier 3 subscriber.

**Targets:** Spend ≈€455, Revenue €655-1,110 (Weeks 2-3 cumulative).

## Phase 4: Sustainability (Days 31-43)

**Stabilisation:** Sustain 2-3 subscriptions (€600-900), complete 1-2 proposals (€800-3,000).

**Retrospective:** Summarize revenue, review health trends, capture lessons, plan Month 2 scaling (€1,500-3,000 target).

**Targets:** Spend ≈€450, Revenue €400-600 (Week 4+ completion).
