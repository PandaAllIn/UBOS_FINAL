# SaaS Benchmarks (B2B ARR €1M-10M)

## Conversion Funnel
- Visitor → signup: 3-6%
- Signup → trial activation: 30-45%
- Trial → paid conversion: 15-25%
- Monthly churn: 1.5-3%

## Financial Metrics
- LTV:CAC ≥ 3:1 (best performers 8-12:1)
- Payback period < 12 months (target 6 months)
- Gross margin 75-90%
- Net revenue retention 90-110%

## Channel Benchmarks (EU consultants)
- SEO/content: CAC €300-500
- LinkedIn ads: CAC €700-1,200
- Webinars/events: CAC €400-900
- Partner/affiliate: CAC €200-400

Sources: OpenView SaaS Benchmarks 2024, Paddle Insights, UBOS Oracle research.
